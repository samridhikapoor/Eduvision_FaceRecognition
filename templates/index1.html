About EduVison


An integrated platform to provide multiple applications including logging in attendance, keeping a check on student's alertness, ensuring a covid free as well as physically secure campus. The needs of an ideal educational institution are ever increasing. With the expansion in aspirations of an institution to grow both numerically as well as area-wise, there is a need to have a single plaftform for provising efficient, accurate and technically sound systems for delivering at fronts - students education as well as security.

  • Log-Me-In : A User-Friendly, Efficient and Cent Percent Reliable Software to LogIn and mark Attendance by Facial Recognition
  • SecureEx ALPR : Automatic License Plate Recognition software that works in all environments and is optimized for your location.
  • MaskEssentia : A Web app to helps users stay safe by detecting whether they are wearing a face mask or not, using camera vision algorithms.
  • SmartAttention : A software that allows teachers and educators to monitor their students' devices in real-time and keep a check that they are alert during lectures.
