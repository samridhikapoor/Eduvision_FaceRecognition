Sleep Detection

It will look for the facial features like "Eye Aspect Ratio" (EAR) that will detect features like closed eyes in between lectures or while performing an activity.

Yawn Detection

It will look for the facial features like "Mouth Aspect Ratio" (MAR) that will detect if a student is yawning during lectures or while performing an activity.

About Eduvison

A software that allows teachers and educators to monitor their students' devices in real-time and keep a check that they are alert during lectures.

The primary source of gaining knowledge is shifting to online courses because online courses are:-

  • economical
  • flexible
  • have plethora of courses to choose from
  • provide comfortable environment
  • the only left source of gaining knowledge in situations like the COVID-19 Pandemic
  • permits everybody to have access to courses conducted by renowned professors from esteemed universities


    • However, online courses are less communicative as compared to classroom teaching. Whenever a teacher finds that the student is inattentive because of drowsiness, they are able to capture the students’ interest back by asking them questions relevant to the topic. In online courses, there is no such provision of two-way communication between the instructor and the attendee. In other words, there is no one to keep a track of the students’ behaviour during the conduct of the lectures. To overcome this issue, SmartAttention software provides the feature of detecting drowsiness of the attendee. The AI based software functions by looking for the facial features like Mouth Aspect Ratio (MAR) and Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR), to detect if the attendee is drowsy or is yawning while the lectures are in progress or while performing an activity.
