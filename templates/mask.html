We Want To Help Out With Covid-19

There is a lot going on in the world right now and we understand the struggles to staying safe while doing everyday activity. Therefore, we created MaskProtech, which is a web app to helps users stay safe by detecting whether they are wearing a face mask or not, using camera vision algorithms.

Live Camera Recognition

Our app uses OpenCV and machine learning to detect whether somebody is wearing a mask and is protected or not. There is no longer a need for slow machine learning algorithms to take place after a picture is taken, now it's all being done live.

Recurring News Feed

Using a Python News API, we were able to make direct calls to our web app and display them in an elegant and neatly nested format. Our responsive news feed page is an easy way to get a quick dose of what's going in on the world, with minimal effort or reading.

MaskEssentia

Due to all the recent news and warnings regarding the Covid-19 Pandemic, we decided to create this web application to help level the playing field because we do understand that not everybody has the time to keep up with everything while maintaing their everyday life. Our web app allows users to instantly see through a live camera if they're wearing a protective mask or not, and they can change their coverings accordingly. This technology has many potential uses in security cameras in public areas as well. We believe that this technology can create a significant impact and enhance the already existing principles of social distancing.
